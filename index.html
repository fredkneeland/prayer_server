Join with us in praying for the persecuted Church!

For the week of April 8th-14th we will be praying for the persecuted church around the clock in 15 minute increments. We invite you to join us and to sign up for a fifteen minute slot each day this week, so that together we can offer up unbroken prayer for the persecuted church.

What can we pray for?

Faith

Pray that their faith will be strengthened through their sufferings.

Witness

Pray for many to come to Christ through the testimony of the suffering church.

Workers

Pray that the Lord of the Harvest would send out workers into the fields.
